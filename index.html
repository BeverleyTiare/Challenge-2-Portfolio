About

Future Directions

A new type of neuroscience is emerging: within the multitude of theorists, teams of neuroscientists that do science with data on neural activity is rising. These teams are not just creating methods for analysing and gathering data - these neuroscientists are implementing the full range of modern computational techniques on data to answer scientific questions about the brain - a Neural data science has now emerged.

As yet, we are not completely conversant of how our brains encode behaviour - so we work with behavioural labels, but we almost always know these are not the complete answer: they are just pathways to possibilities.

In turning to Systems neuroscience we are offered an expansive playground for those who can fuse their knowledge of neuroscience to their skill of data analysis, opening the door to Neural data science.

Lets delve a bit further...a Neural data scientist asks scientific questions about data from Systems neuroscience to reveal how neurons work together to do what they do - and right now, there are ways we can begin to answer that question: by looking at established classes of problems in computational challenges in Systems neuroscience. So, if you are interested in discovering more, connect in the "get in touch" form.
